Career Pathways
Potential Employment

Where This Certificate Takes You

Most IT careers begin in technical support. This certificate is the fastest route to a real IT role and a launchpad to much more.

IT Support Technician

Helps users fix computer, software, internet,email, and system issues. This is one of the best entry level IT roles.
Estimated Salary: R170,000 - R185,000 per year

Desktop Support Technician

Focuses on supporting desktop and laptop computers, operating systems, user accounts, including hardware troubleshooting, software installation, updates, and user support.
Estimated Salary: R180,000 - R240,000 per year

Help Desk / Service Desk Technician

First person users contact for IT issues, providing remote or in-person support for a wide range of technical problems, including software, hardware, and network troubleshooting.
Estimated Salary:R140,000 - R190,000 per year

Junior Systems Administrator

Assists with maintaining and supporting an organization's IT infrastructure, including servers, networks, and user accounts, often a stepping stone to more advanced IT roles.
Estimated Salary: R180,000 - R260,000 per year

Network Operations Center (NOC) Technician

Monitors and maintains an organization's IT infrastructure, including networks, servers, and applications, ensuring optimal performance and quick resolution of any issues that arise.
Estimated Salary: R155,000 - R230,000 per year
